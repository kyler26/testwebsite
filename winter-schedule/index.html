Winter Schedule

Our winter schedule can be seen below. For outdoor runs we split into short and long groups. Shorter runs are usually 1 - 3.5 miles and long runs are 3.5 - 6 miles.

Indoor runs are usually more speed/workout focused due to limited space in the rec center.

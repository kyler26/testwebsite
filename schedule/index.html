Schedule

Our weekly schedule can be seen below. At most runs we split into short and long groups. Shorter runs are usually 1 - 3.5 miles and long runs are 3.5 - 6 miles. Everyone is welcome regardless of ability!

Spring and Fall

Day Time Location
Mon - Thurs 5PM Meet at the goat on the quad
Fun Friday 5PM Check slack or the weekly email!
Saturday 10AM Meet at the goat on the quad
  • Saturday runs are longer lengths (6+ miles)
  • Wednesdays we hold track workouts instead of our usual runs
  • Fun Fridays are lots of fun activities like team dinners, yoga, lawn games, ice cream runs, movie nights, relays, game nights, and more!

Gym Credit

All WPI students are required to obtain gym credit in order to graduate. Through club sports we are able to offer gym credit to individuals who are registered for WPE 1221 on Workday! At least 21 runs / events must be attended throughout the semester in order to get credit for the class.

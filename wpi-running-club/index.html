WPI Running Club

We are a club that promotes and participates in recreational running at WPI. Everyone regardless of running ability is welcomed!

Our club was founded in the Spring of 2013 and became a member of NIRCA in 2024. The purpose of the Running Club is to bring together WPI students who are interested in running and provide an environment which supports personal growth and camaraderie. We host weekly runs, fun activities, and participate in NIRCA XC & track races. We strive to make the club inclusive and provide opportunities for all running abilities.

Join Us

Below you can find information on how to join Running Club and some frequently asked questions!

Quick Facts

  • We meet most days of the week for group runs around Worcester!
  • We have over 80 active members, (runs are usually anywhere from ~15 to 35 members).
  • We welcome runners of all skill levels.
  • Attendance is not mandatory, show up and run whenever you want to!
  • We also participate in cross country races and track meets throughout the year!

How to join

Welcome! We recommend joining both our email list and slack to get all of our updates. You can also join our club on myWPI if you would like, but please still join the slack and email list.

Button for email list and slack here 

There are also a few forms that must be completed before you can run with us. These are required for all members, regardless of how many runs you attend.

  • Club Sports Liability Waver
  • Concussion Training (Instructions here)
  • Anti-Hazing Form

You can find all of these forms under Club Sports on myWPI. If you need help finding them, let us know on slack or send us an email at running-execs@wpi.edu.
